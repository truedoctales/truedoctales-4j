## The Tale

Jorinde and Joringel are deeply in love. One evening, they wander too close to an evil fairy's castle in the enchanted forest. The fairy transforms Jorinde into a nightingale and locks her in a cage with seven thousand other birds! Joringel is frozen, unable to move or speak.

Can Joringel find the magic blood-red flower with a pearl at its center - the only thing that can break the fairy's spell and free his beloved?
